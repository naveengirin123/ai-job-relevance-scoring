Flipkart’s End of Season Sale (EOSS) is one of the largest and most data-intensive retail events in India, generating millions of transactions, customer interactions, and operational insights. For data analysts aiming to crack 20L+ CTC roles, understanding how to analyze such high-impact business datasets is a must-have skill.

 

In this live, hands-on session, Sumit Shukla — Director & AI Head at ScalerTrix.ai and a seasoned industry expert — walks you through how real Flipkart-scale data is analyzed to drive revenue, pricing, inventory, and customer growth decisions. This session is designed as a reality check for aspiring analysts, showing what companies like Flipkart actually expect beyond textbook analytics.

 

You’ll work with a Flipkart End of Season Sales dataset to understand how analysts uncover insights related to sales performance, demand spikes, discount effectiveness, category-wise growth, and customer buying behavior. The session focuses on thinking like a business analyst, not just writing queries or creating charts.

 

What You’ll Learn

 

  • How large e-commerce companies analyze seasonal sales data

  • Key metrics used during Flipkart’s End of Season Sales

  • Identifying revenue drivers, loss-makers, and growth opportunities

  • Customer behavior analysis during high-discount events

  • Translating raw data into business recommendations

  • How these analytics skills map to 20L+ CTC data roles

  • How to present EOSS-style projects in interviews and portfolios

 

Session Outcome

 

 

By the end of this session, you’ll know how to approach real e-commerce sales datasets with confidence, extract high-impact insights, and communicate them like a professional analyst. You’ll leave with a portfolio-ready understanding of Flipkart-style data problems and a clear roadmap to prepare for high-paying data roles.
